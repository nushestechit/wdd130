Overview

Purpose

My website will help students learn how to play the piano, which can help them develop their creativity, improve their cognitive abilities, and enhance their memory. I will also provide resources such as sheet music, instructional videos, and practice exercises to help you learn at their own pace.

Audience

My target customers are 10 years old and above with medium income. They love to play instruments and be creative. They are looking for cheap content that can help them learn how to play instruments without having to pay for expensive tutors. My target audience needs content that is affordable and accessible.
